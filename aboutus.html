About Us

Rooted in Family, Inspired by the Sea

Bada Bébé was founded in 2023 by mother Jordean Ulysses after a trip to the Korean seaside with her son - Stink.
She had noticed that there were many boutiques that would offer kidswear and mothercare separately, but never items for both mother and child in one place.
The name "Bada" means "Sea" in Korean and also happens to be her nephew's childhood nickname.
So after speaking to her family, and doing some research- Bada Bébé was established.
At Bada Bébé we specialise in Korean style kidswear for children aged 0-6 and although based in London, UK
prides itself on sourcing and giving back to the very place it's inspired by - South Korea.
